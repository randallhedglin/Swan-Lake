Audio Settings

These settings control the playback of music and sound effects while the screensaver is running. Options include enabling of music, sound, and 3-D sound effects, as well as a series of volume controls.

Music: Check this box to enable music playback. The screensaver plays “Le Cygne (The Swan)” by Camille Saint-Saens. The default value is “on”.

Sound Effects: Check this box to enable playback of sound effects. A number of sound effects are played depending on the current environment settings. The default value is “on”.

3-D Sound: Check this box to enable rendering of sound effects in “3-D spatializing” mode. If your audio hardware does not support this feature, the application will run more slowly with 3-D sound enabled. The default value is “on”.

Master Volume: This slider controls the combined music and sound effect volume. Slide to the left to decrease volume and to the right to increase.

Music Volume: This slider controls the relative volume of music playback while the screensaver is running. Slide to the left to decrease volume and to the right to increase.

Sound Volume: This slider controls the relative volume of sound-effect playback while the screensaver is running. Slide to the left to decrease volume and to the right to increase.
